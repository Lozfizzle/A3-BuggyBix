What is Aperture

How blurred your image appears.

The aperture of the camera lens controls how much light reaches the sensor or film, as is represented by the letter f.

Large Aperture

Low f number (f2.8)

A large aperture is represented by a low f number, and allows more light through the lens. This creates a blurry background

Small Aperture

High f number (f22)

A small aperture is represented by a high f number, and allows less light through the lens. This creates a sharp background.
